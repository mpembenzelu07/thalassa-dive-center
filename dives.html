Our Dive Sites

Explore the best diving locations around Dar es Salaam

Coral Garden

Beautiful shallow reef perfect for beginners and photographers. Vibrant soft and hard corals with abundant reef fish.

Depth: 5-12m Beginner Friendly Great Visibility

Shark Point

Dramatic wall dive with frequent shark sightings. Vertical drop-off covered in colorful sponges and black corals.

Depth: 15-30m Advanced Level Shark Encounters

Turtle Bay

Consistent turtle sightings in this protected bay. Gentle sloping reef perfect for all levels of divers.

Depth: 8-18m All Levels Turtle Heaven
