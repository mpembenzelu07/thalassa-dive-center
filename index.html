Your Gateway to Underwater Adventure

Thalassa Dive Center is a PADI-registered dive operator located on the Msasani/Slipway Peninsula of Dar es Salaam. We offer diving and water sport experiences for both beginners and advanced divers.

Our certified instructors provide personalized training in a safe, fun environment. Whether you're taking your first breath underwater or exploring advanced dive sites, we ensure an unforgettable experience.
