Booking Information

Common questions about booking and preparing for your dive

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (June-October). For PADI courses, 2-3 weeks notice is ideal to ensure availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48+ hours before departure receive a full refund. 24-48 hours notice receives 50% refund. Less than 24 hours notice is non-refundable.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes, we require a 30% deposit to confirm your booking. The balance is payable on the day of your dive or course.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (USD/TZS), credit cards (Visa/Mastercard), and bank transfers. Credit card payments incur a 3% processing fee.
