Booking Information

Common questions about booking and preparing for your dive with Thalassa

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance, especially for PADI courses. Weekends and holidays book up quickly, so 1-2 weeks notice is ideal during peak seasons.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48+ hours before departure receive a full refund. 24-48 hours notice receives 50% refund. Less than 24 hours notice is non-refundable due to boat and guide arrangements.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes, we require a 30% deposit to confirm your booking, payable via mobile money or bank transfer. The balance is payable on the day of your dive or course at our center.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (TZS/USD), mobile money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money), and bank transfers. Credit card payments may be arranged in advance with a small processing fee.

Do you provide equipment?

Yes, all necessary diving equipment is included in our dive packages and courses. We have modern, well-maintained gear in all sizes. Equipment rental is also available separately.

What should I bring?

Swimwear, towel, sunscreen (reef-safe preferred), and any personal medication. We provide drinking water. For full-day trips, you may want to bring snacks or we can arrange lunch.
