Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your snorkeling adventure in Dar es Salaam

Do I need to know how to swim to snorkel?

Basic swimming ability is recommended but not required for our family tours. We provide life vests for all participants and have beginner-friendly tours in shallow, calm waters where non-swimmers can comfortably enjoy snorkeling with guidance from our instructors.

What is the minimum age for snorkeling tours?

Children as young as 6 can join our family-friendly tours with parental supervision. We have special equipment for children and our guides are trained to work with young snorkelers. For more adventurous tours, the minimum age is 12 years.

What should I bring for a snorkeling tour?

We recommend bringing: swimwear, towel, reef-safe sunscreen, hat, sunglasses, change of clothes, and any personal medication. We provide all snorkeling equipment, drinking water, and snacks. Full-day tours include lunch.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48+ hours before departure receive a full refund. 24-48 hours notice receives 50% refund. Less than 24 hours notice is non-refundable. We monitor weather conditions and will notify you if tours need to be rescheduled due to safety concerns.

Are there any medical restrictions?

Snorkeling is generally safe for most people. However, if you have serious medical conditions (heart problems, severe asthma, epilepsy, etc.), please consult your doctor before booking. Pregnant women can join our calm water tours but should avoid more adventurous tours.
