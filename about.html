Our Story

For over 15 years, Thalassa Dive Center has been sharing the wonders of Tanzania's underwater world with adventurers from around the globe.

Passion for the Ocean Since 2005

Founded by marine biologists Ali Hassan and Sarah Johnson, Thalassa Dive Center began as a small operation with one boat and a dream: to share the incredible marine biodiversity of Tanzania while promoting ocean conservation.

What started as a passion project has grown into Tanzania's most respected dive center, operating in both Zanzibar and Mafia Island. We've trained thousands of divers and introduced even more people to the magic of snorkeling.

Our commitment to safety, education, and environmental stewardship has earned us recognition from PADI and numerous conservation organizations.
