About Thalassa Dive Center

Dar es Salaam's premier PADI-registered dive center, bringing underwater adventures to the heart of Tanzania's coastal capital.

Your Gateway to Dar es Salaam's Underwater World

Located at Shop 17C in the vibrant Maga Beach/Slipway area of Masaki, Thalassa Dive Center has established itself as Dar es Salaam's go-to destination for diving and water sports enthusiasts.

As a PADI-registered dive operator, we bring professional diving experiences to both beginners and advanced divers right in the heart of Tanzania's coastal capital. Our prime location on the Msasani/Slipway Peninsula provides easy access to Dar es Salaam's best dive sites and island destinations.

We're proud to maintain a 4.3-star rating from our growing community of divers, reflecting our commitment to safety, quality instruction, and unforgettable underwater experiences.
